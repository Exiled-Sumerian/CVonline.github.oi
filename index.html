About

Welcome to my webpage of FREE downloadable CV templates!

Whether you’re a school leaver, a sales executive, or re-entering the workforce, I have a CV template just for you!

I noticed that many CV templates online are locked behind paywalls—where money can be tight during your time as a job seeker. My goal is to help you stand out from the sea of applicants with tailored, easily editable CVs.

These FREE templates come in .DOC format, making them simple to customize using Google Docs, Microsoft Word, Pages, and more. Each template includes sections for your job search, skills, education, and personal details.

In the future, I will design more CV templates and cover letters, and other resources to support you in your job search.
